About StarIntel Gserver 

prev | up | next | toc

Starintel-gserver is a processing framework for starintel documents. it was created after a need that a mess of random scripts, bots and a database wasnt enough. I needed something to allow multiple bots to communicate, store, query and handle new targets. As of <2024-09-04 Wed> This is the second iteration of the starintel service. This version improves on starRouter with actors instead of what i called actors, are really just consumers in star-gserver. Gserver started in a org-mode notebook while i scketched things out.
